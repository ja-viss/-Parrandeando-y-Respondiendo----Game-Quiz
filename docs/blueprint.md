## Core Features:

- Question Generator: Generate quiz questions focused exclusively on Venezuelan Christmas traditions, including gastronomy, music, and customs. Use a tool for when to focus on each category of knowledge.
- Solo Play Mode: A single-player mode where users can test their knowledge against the clock and track their high scores.
- Group Competition Mode: Multiplayer mode that allows up to four players to compete simultaneously, with a live leaderboard.
- Real-Time Leaderboard: Display a leaderboard in real-time, updating as players answer questions, to create a competitive environment during group play.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich red (#C70039) to evoke the warmth and vibrancy of Venezuelan Christmas traditions.
- Background color: Soft off-white (#F2E9E4) to create a cozy and inviting atmosphere.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFC300) to highlight important elements and reflect the festive spirit.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for an elegant and festive title feel; body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability and a modern look.
- Use custom icons representing traditional Venezuelan Christmas symbols, such as hallacas, musical instruments, and nativity scenes.
- Incorporate subtle celebratory animations upon correct answers and mode transitions.
- Design should evoke a sense of tradition and fun, with a layout that is familiar to users.
